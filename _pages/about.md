I am an Integrated Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Jung Hee Cheon. My research focuses on **homomorphic encryption (HE)** and its applications to **privacy-preserving machine learning** — building cryptographic systems that let computation happen directly on encrypted data without ever decrypting it.
